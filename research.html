Research

My research spans materials for information storage, energy harvesting, and sensing — from amorphous chalcogenide glasses to perovskite photovoltaics and self-powered nanogenerators.

Research Areas

  • Chalcogenide-based materials for phase-change memory
  • Photovoltaic cells for energy harvesting (perovskite, CZTS/kesterite, lead-free structures)
  • Self-powered high-performance piezo/tribo/pyro-electric nanogenerators (PENGs/TENGs/PyENGs)
  • Surface plasmon resonance (SPR) sensors and actuators
  • Advanced 2D nanomaterials and devices for energy harvesting, sensing, and storage
